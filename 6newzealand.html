1.Which famous film series was filmed against the backdrop of New Zealand's stunning landscapes?

The "Lord of the Rings" and "The Hobbit" film series were filmed in New Zealand, showcasing its breathtaking natural beauty.

2.What is the indigenous language of New Zealand, and what is its significance?

The indigenous language is Te Reo Māori, spoken by the Māori people. It holds cultural importance and is an official language of New Zealand.

3.What activity is New Zealand often referred to as the "Adventure Capital of the World"

New Zealand is known as the "Adventure Capital of the World" due to its wide range of adventurous activities, such as bungee jumping, skydiving, and white-water rafting.

4. What is a notable bird species and national symbol of New Zealand?

The kiwi bird is a notable species and a national symbol of New Zealand. It's flightless, nocturnal, and has become synonymous with the country's identity..

5.What is the Fiordland National Park famous for, and where is it located?

Fiordland National Park, located on the South Island, is renowned for its breathtaking fjords, including Milford Sound and Doubtful Sound, offering some of New Zealand's most spectacular natural landscapes..
