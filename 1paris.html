1.What is the Eiffel Tower?

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron landmark in Paris, France, built in 1889 for the World's Fair. It's an iconic symbol of the city and a popular tourist attraction..

2.Which famous art museum is located in Paris?

The Louvre Museum is located in Paris and is one of the world's largest and most renowned art museums, housing a vast collection of artworks and historical artifacts.

3.What is the significance of the Champs-Élysées?

The Champs-Élysées is a famous avenue in Paris known for its shops, theaters, and cafes. It's often associated with prestigious events, parades, and serves as a cultural and historical landmark.

4. What is Montmartre known for?

Montmartre is a historic district in Paris known for its artistic heritage, including being a gathering place for many famous artists. It's also home to the Basilica of the Sacred Heart (Sacre-Cœur).

5.What is the Paris Metro?

The Paris Metro is the city's extensive subway system, providing convenient transportation throughout the city. It's one of the oldest metro systems in the world and an essential part of Parisian life. ....
