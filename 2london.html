1.What is the River Thames known for in London?

The River Thames flows through London and is famous for its iconic landmarks, such as the Tower Bridge and the London Eye, which offer stunning views of the city's skyline..

2.Which famous residence is situated in the heart of London?

Buckingham Palace, the official residence of the British monarch, is located in central London and serves as a symbol of the British monarchy.

3.What is the significance of the British Museum in London?

The British Museum is renowned for its vast collection of art and artifacts from around the world, showcasing human history and culture across millennia..

4.Which area in London is celebrated for its theatre scene?

The West End of London is famous for its vibrant theatre district, featuring numerous theaters that host a wide range of plays, musicals, and performances.

5. What event takes place annually on the River Thames in London?

The Oxford and Cambridge Boat Race, a historic rowing competition between the two prestigious universities, occurs on the River Thames, drawing crowds to watch this intense rivalry.
